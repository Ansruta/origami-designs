Interesting facts about a Chameleon

  • Part of the iguan suborders
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communicating among the chameleons
  • Most chameleons have prereptile tails used to wrap around tree branches

Interesting facts about a Pigeon

  • Are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • Known for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Have exellent reading abilities

Interesting facts about a Cicada

  • They are true bugs
  • Cicadas don’t sing at night
  • They improve lawns by digging tunnels that allow air into the soil. When they die, their rotting bodies put nitrogen into the soil.

Interesting facts about a Panda

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!
  • Pandas like to lick copper and iron.
